Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy allows you to cancel your trip up to 48 hours before your scheduled departure for a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours of departure may incur a fee.

Are there any travel restrictions I should be aware of?

Travel restrictions can vary based on your destination and current global health situations. We recommend checking the official government websites for the latest information before booking your trip.

What documents do I need to bring with me?

Documents needed for travel will be a valid passport. It will also be important to bring your government issued ID just in case. Make sure your name on your ticket matches the name on your ID and passport

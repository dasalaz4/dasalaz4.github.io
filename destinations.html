Destinations

Mexico

Enjoy the warm tropical weather of Mexico. Throughout every corner of the country, there is a story to be told. Take a walk down the markets filled with handmade textiles and the aroma of fresh street tacos, or wander down the city centers and enjoy the beautiful architecture and festivals Mexico has to offer.

Italy

Italy is a country where everywhere you go feels as if it belongs in a painting. From the starlit canals of Venice to the giant and majestic Colosseum, there is no rush to take in the timeless beauty around you. Visit the many museums Italy has to offer and enjoy the history before you.
